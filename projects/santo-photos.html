What is Santo Photos?

Santo Photos is the home base for the photography of Santo Fothergill. It's built to do three jobs at once, cleanly: show the work, sell the work, and book the work. Rather than scatter a portfolio across social media, a booking form on one service, and a print shop on another, everything lives together in a single fast, focused platform — the same lightweight philosophy behind every Fothergill project, applied to a creative business.

For visitors, it's a gallery you can lose an hour in. For clients, it's the place to hire a photographer and receive their images. For fellow photographers, it's a shop for the editing presets that give the work its look. The goal was to make each of those paths obvious and frictionless, so the software gets out of the way and the photographs do the talking.

Why it was built

Working photographers usually end up duct-taping their business together from half a dozen tools that were never meant to talk to each other. A portfolio host here, a booking calendar there, a print-fulfillment service somewhere else, and a preset marketplace that takes a cut of every sale. Santo Photos was built to replace that patchwork with one platform that's fast, cohesive, and fully owned — no per-sale middlemen deciding how the work is presented, and no slow, template-heavy page keeping visitors waiting. It's a photographer's business, run on a photographer's terms.

What you can do on Santo Photos

Browse curated collections

The portfolio is organized into collections so you can explore the work by theme or project rather than scrolling an endless feed. Images are optimized to load quickly even on a phone connection, so the browsing experience stays smooth.

Book a session

If you want to hire Santo, the platform lets you request and arrange a photography session directly — no back-and-forth across three different apps. It's the front door for portraits, events, and commissioned work.

Buy fine-art prints

Favorite images are available as prints, so a photograph you love on screen can end up on your wall. Ordering runs through a clean, straightforward checkout.

Download editing presets

For photographers who want to work in a similar style, Santo Photos sells editing presets — the color and tone recipes behind the look of the work — as a downloadable product.

Getting the most out of it

  • Start with the collections to get a feel for the style before booking or buying — they're the clearest window into the work.
  • Note the details when booking — a little context about your session helps the whole thing go smoothly.
  • Presets are a shortcut, not a substitute — they get you close to the look, and then your own eye takes it the rest of the way.
  • Prints reward good sources — the images are prepared for print quality, so they hold up beautifully at size.

Frequently asked questions

Can I browse without buying anything?

Absolutely. The collections are free to explore — buying prints or presets and booking sessions are optional.

How do I book a photography session?

Use the booking option on the Santo Photos site to request and arrange a session directly.

What are the presets?

They're editing presets — downloadable color and tone settings that reproduce the look of the work in your own photo editor.

Do I need an account?

Browsing needs no account. You may create one when placing an order or booking so you can track it.

How do I get in touch about custom work?

Reach out through the booking or contact options on Santo Photos, or via the Fothergill contact page.
