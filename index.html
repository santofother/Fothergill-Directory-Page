About Us

Independent, lightweight, and built to last

Fothergill is a small independent studio focused on one thing: building lightweight, dependable web apps, interactive games, and custom tool engineering that people can use directly in the browser — no downloads, no bloated frameworks, no unnecessary accounts.

Each project on this site — from the bluffing mechanics behind Liar's Dice, to the trivia-driven hand-building in Trivia Poker, to the budget-and-read strategy behind NFL Budget Draft, to the ranking-and-guessing rounds in Do You Know Me?, to the curation tools running Santo Photos — is designed, coded, and maintained independently. We favor clean interfaces, fast load times, and tools that respect the visitor's time.

This hub exists as the central directory for all active Fothergill systems, so every project has one reliable home.

Privacy Policy

How we handle data and advertising

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies and web beacons to collect non-personally identifiable information to provide targeted advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

This site does not sell personally identifiable information. Individual project subdomains linked from this hub (such as Liar's Dice, Poker Platform, and Santo Photos) may maintain their own separate privacy practices relevant to their specific functionality.

If you would like to opt out of personalized advertising, you can adjust your preferences through your browser settings or through your advertising provider's opt-out tools.
