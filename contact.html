Get in touch

Bug report, feature idea, a question about one of the projects, or a photography booking — it all reaches the same inbox, and it's genuinely read.

Email us directly

The fastest way to reach Fothergill is by email. Messages typically get a reply within a couple of business days.

contact@fothergill.com

What's helpful to include

  • Bug reports: which project, what you did, what happened, and the device and browser you were using. That context is the difference between a quick fix and a mystery.
  • Feature ideas: what you'd like to see and, if you can, why — the "why" often shapes a better solution than the specific request.
  • Photography: for bookings, prints, or presets, mention what you have in mind and you'll be pointed to the right place on Santo Photos.

Common questions

How quickly will I hear back?

Usually within a couple of business days. Detailed bug reports sometimes take a little longer if they need investigating, but they do get looked at.

Do you take feature requests?

Yes. Not every idea makes it in, but real feedback genuinely shapes what gets built and fixed next.

Who am I actually emailing?

Fothergill is an independent studio run by Santo Fothergill, so your message goes straight to the person who builds and maintains the projects.
